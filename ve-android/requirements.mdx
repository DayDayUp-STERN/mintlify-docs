## Minimum System Requirements

To successfully integrate and run the Banuba Video Editor SDK, ensure your development environment and target devices meet the following requirements:

### Development Environment
- **Android Studio**: Arctic Fox (2020.3.1) or higher
- **Kotlin**: 1.9.0 or higher
- **Gradle**: 7.3.0 or higher

### Target Device Requirements
- **Android API Level**: 23+ (Android 6.0 or higher)
- **OpenGL ES**: 3.0 or higher
- **Architecture**: arm64-v8a, armeabi-v7a, x86, x86_64

### Recommended Specifications
For the best user experience, we suggest:
- **RAM**: 4GB or more
- **CPU**: Snapdragon 8 series, Exynos 9 series, or equivalent
- **Android Version**: 10.0 or higher

<Note>
The SDK supports most Android devices released after 2018. Older devices may experience performance issues with certain effects and features.
</Note>

### Storage Requirements
- **SDK Size**: ~220MB in a release build (can vary based on included features)
- **Runtime Storage**: At least 500MB of free storage is recommended for temporary files during video processing

<Warning>
For devices with less than 3GB of RAM, some advanced features like certain AR effects and high-resolution exports may be limited to ensure stable performance.
</Warning>
