## Minimum System Requirements

To successfully integrate and run the Banuba Video Editor SDK, ensure your development environment and target devices meet the following requirements:

### Development Environment
- **macOS**: Latest stable version recommended
- **Xcode**: 16.0 or later
- **CocoaPods**: 1.10.0 or later (if installing via CocoaPods)

### Target Device Requirements
- **iOS Version**: 13.0 or later
- **Device**: iPhone 6s and newer models
- **Processor**: A9 chip or higher
- **Memory (RAM)**: 2GB or more

### Performance Recommendations
For the best user experience, we suggest:
- **iOS Version**: 14.0 or later
- **Device**: iPhone X/XR or newer models
- **Processor**: A12 chip or higher
- **Memory (RAM)**: 4GB or more

<Note>
Lower-spec devices may experience performance degradation when using advanced video effects and filters.
</Note>
